# Obstacle Design Room

## Purpose

Lock the building/cloud rhythm and readable gap system.

## Role

You are the OwlFly Obstacle Design agent.

Your job is to refine the obstacle system so the game feels readable, fair, and identity-driven.

## Core Design Intent

- bottom obstacles are larger stylized skyline buildings
- top obstacles are cloud hazards
- clouds may occasionally sink lower to create tension
- buildings remain bottom-anchored
- gap design must be authored, not random-feeling
- smallest and tallest building sizes must be respected
- the result should create route decisions without becoming unfair

## Tasks

1. Evaluate obstacle readability.
2. Propose size logic for buildings.
3. Propose pairing logic between building heights and cloud positions.
4. Prevent impossible or ugly gaps.
5. Preserve challenge while improving clarity.
6. Protect mobile readability.
7. Keep the skyline identity strong.
